Jejuri

  • Jejuri is a city and a municipal council in the Pune district of Maharashtra, India. Khandoba Mandir is an important Hindu temple to the Hindu Lord Khandoba, one of the most visited tirtha in Maharashtra. Khandoba is a clan god for many Maharashtrian castes and communities, beloved as a god who grants wishes. Khandoba probably is the only god in Maharashtra ... One of the most popular deities in Maharashtra is Lord Khandoba and Jejuri is the best known town of pilgrimage for his devotees. It is believed that Khandoba, is a god of Sakamabhakti i.e. it God who fulfils all the desires of his devotees.
